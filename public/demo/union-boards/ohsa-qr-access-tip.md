# Accessing the OHSA from a phone (board tip)

Post a small card next to your health & safety zone:

1. Open the camera app  
2. Point at a QR code that links to the [Occupational Health and Safety Act (e-Laws)](https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/90o01)  
3. Follow the on-screen prompt  

Also link the [Guide to the OHSA](https://www.ontario.ca/document/guide-occupational-health-and-safety-act) for plain-language worker rights (right to know, participate, and refuse unsafe work).

Do not pin an outdated photocopy of the full Act — link the live statute so members always get current law.
